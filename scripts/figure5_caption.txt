(e)~Failure-mode matrix showing projection error amplification when individual invariants are perturbed in isolation. At Break intensity, $\rho_{\text{drift}}$ distortion induces control collapse (45$\times$ error amplification), while $\rho_{\text{queue}}$ saturation triggers a phase transition (50$\times$), demonstrating that PRESS validity depends on preserving \emph{both} pressure and control invariants.
